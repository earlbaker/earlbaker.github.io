about Earl's Professional Qualifications


Earl has the experience and knowledge to successfully represent you as a consultant in government relations. His extensive public career has given him the opportunity to work in many leadership positions and to work with many decision makers and officials.

Knowing how government works at all levels is a key ingredient in what makes Earl Baker effective in government relations. Whether the policy issue or procurement process in which you are interested is federal, state, county or municipal, Earl knows the territory.

Earl has combined the elements of his career to represent clients who need to be recognized in the world of government. His current clients are in the fields of Information Technology, Healthcare and Engineering, and his practice will accommodate a number of varied industries and interests. He works with government people at all levels, in legislative and executive branches, and both parties, to assist you in accomplishing your goals.
