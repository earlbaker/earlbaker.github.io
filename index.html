My public affairs career is based on using my experience and knowledge to serve people, and I welcome you to my website.

Earl Baker Consulting is now in its eighth year, successfully advocating for clients in the healthcare, information technology and engineering fields.

From time to time I post blog entries about my recent experiences.

An interesting and fulfilling segment of my career was spent in the trenches of local and state politics, and I am sharing my advice on how to run a winning campaign and get elected. My popular “how to” set of 4 CD’s, “Run To Win! How To Successfully Run and Win A Local or State Election” is now a book as well so it can be purchased in either (or both) formats. You may prefer the affordable paperback, or the ebook version is very reasonable and accessible. You can check out both in the Books section of this website.

Of course my Heroes & Hypocrites book of humorous verse about politics continues to be of interest and is also on the Books section of this website. Please take a moment to read my sample poem, “On Account.” It will give you the flavor of these poems!

The Executive Leaders Program, a class for top executives and upwardly mobile executives about politics and government in Pennsylvania, has fifty alumni and has reached Class VI. The program after the current one will be in Fall, 2013.
